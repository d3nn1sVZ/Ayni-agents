NOTE FOR OPERATORS: this output captures the verified L402 unlock flow
(steps 1-3) which we ran end-to-end on Bitcoin mainnet during the
hackathon. Step 4 (the onward redistribution to contributor wallets)
runs identically once the agent-wallet is funded with enough sats to
cover both the L402 payment AND the sum of contributor shares. For
data-science-es that means at least 150 sats; for tributario-pe at
least 200. Each contributor's lnAddress is configured in
data/tribus.json — for the demo all share one BOLT12 sink so payments
loop back to the same wallet and prove the mechanism end-to-end on
mainnet without needing 8 separate funded wallets standing by.
